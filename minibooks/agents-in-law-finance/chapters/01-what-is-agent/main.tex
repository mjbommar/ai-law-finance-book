\begin{highlightbox}[title={Chapter Overview}]
This chapter establishes the conceptual foundations for understanding agents and agentic systems. We synthesize seven decades of scholarship across philosophy, psychology, law, economics, and computer science into a unified three-level framework: \textbf{Agent} (minimal agency), \textbf{Agentic System} (operational agency), and \textbf{Agentic AI} (AI-powered agency).

You will learn to evaluate any system---human, organizational, or computational---using the six-property framework: \textbf{Goals}, \textbf{Perception}, \textbf{Action}, \textbf{Iteration}, \textbf{Adaptation}, and \textbf{Termination}.
\end{highlightbox}
