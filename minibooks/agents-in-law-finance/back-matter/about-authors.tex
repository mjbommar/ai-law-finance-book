Michael J. Bommarito II is CEO of 273 Ventures and Adjunct Professor of Law at Michigan State University, where his research applies machine learning and natural language processing to legal practice. He co-founded LexPredict (acquired 2018) and led the research demonstrating GPT-4's ability to pass the Uniform Bar Examination. His work has appeared in \textit{Science}, \textit{PLOS ONE}, and \textit{Artificial Intelligence \& Law}, and he is the editor of \textit{Legal Informatics} (Cambridge University Press, 2021).
\end{tcolorbox}

Daniel M. Katz is Professor of Law at Illinois Tech Chicago-Kent College of Law, Director of The Law Lab, and Academic Director of the Bucerius Center for Legal Technology and Data Science. Named by the \textit{Financial Times} as one of the top 20 legal market shapers of the past two decades, he co-founded 273 Ventures and LexPredict. He holds a Ph.D. in political science and public policy from the University of Michigan and a J.D. \textit{cum laude} from Michigan Law.
