Michael is a serial entrepreneur and researcher whose work spans artificial intelligence, law, and finance. As CEO of 273 Ventures and Adjunct Professor at Michigan State University College of Law, he has published nearly fifty articles in venues including \textit{Science} and Cambridge University Press, with his research cited over three thousand times. He co-founded LexPredict (acquired 2018) and led the research demonstrating GPT-4's ability to pass the Uniform Bar Examination.
