\section*{How to Read This Chapter}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{How to Read This Chapter}

Focus on the sections aligned to your data: PDFs and layout for filings/contracts, tables/charts for financials, audio/transcripts for calls.

\begin{keybox}[title={Key Objectives}]
\begin{itemize}
  \item Preserve document structure and identifiers (pages, figures, tables).
  \item Extract tables/charts accurately and capture units/footnotes.
  \item Summarize audio with timestamps and speaker labels.
  \item Apply privacy/redaction safeguards before external processing.
\end{itemize}
\end{keybox}
