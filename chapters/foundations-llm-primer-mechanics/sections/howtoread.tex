\section*{How to Read This Chapter}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{How to Read This Chapter}

This chapter is a plain-English primer. It gives you a mental model for modern LLMs without math. If you only have 15 minutes, skim the \emph{Key Objectives} box and read Sections~\ref{sec:llmA-history} and \ref{sec:llmA-tokens}.

\begin{keybox}[title={Key Objectives}]
\begin{itemize}
  \item Understand what LLMs are and why they improved so quickly.
  \item Know what tokens, context windows, and sampling do in practice.
  \item Recognize common failure modes you must guard against later.
\end{itemize}
\end{keybox}
