\section{Industry Implementation Patterns and Integrations}
\label{sec:agents2-industry}

We survey common deployment patterns for legal agents and key integration touchpoints (DMS, research platforms, practice management, calendaring/filing systems), highlighting trade-offs and anti-patterns.

\subsection{Common Topologies}
\label{sec:agents2-topologies}
Single-agent with tools; orchestrated multi-agent with shared memory; hub-and-spoke integrations with standardized capability gateways.

\subsection{Key Integrations}
\label{sec:agents2-integrations}
Document management, research/citators, identity/SSO, and workflow/orchestration layers. Prefer API-first vendors and portable workflows.
