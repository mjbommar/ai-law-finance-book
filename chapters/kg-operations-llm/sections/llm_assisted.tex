\section{LLM-Assisted Construction and Curation}
\label{sec:kg-ops-llm}

LLMs can propose entities, relations, and candidate axioms from text, tables, and schemas; they can also draft SPARQL queries and SHACL shapes. Keep humans-in-the-loop and gate changes through validation and provenance capture. Surveys highlight promise and current limits for schema-precise extraction. \autocite{survey-llm-kg-2024}

\begin{definitionbox}[title={Human-in-the-Loop Gates}]
\begin{itemize}
  \item Candidate triples and shapes must pass SHACL and reviewer checks before merge.
  \item Keep suggested vs. accepted changes separate for measurement.
\end{itemize}
\end{definitionbox}
