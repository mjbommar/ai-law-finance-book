\section{Provenance and Versioning}
\label{sec:kg-ops-provenance}

Use PROV-O to record ``who/what/when/how'' for dataset creation, extraction runs, human curation, and publication. Align agent roles and timestamps with your audit requirements. \autocite{w3c-provo}

\begin{keybox}[title={Minimal Provenance Pattern}]
\begin{itemize}
  \item Activities for ingestion, mapping, validation; entities for inputs/outputs; agents for systems and reviewers.
  \item Version IRIs for datasets and vocabularies; maintain change logs.
\end{itemize}
\end{keybox}
