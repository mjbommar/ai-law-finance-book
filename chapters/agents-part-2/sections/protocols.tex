\section{Protocols for Safe Interoperation}
\label{sec:agents2-protocols}

Agents rely on interoperable protocols to discover capabilities and invoke tools safely under uncertainty. We outline design requirements and legal-domain extensions for modern protocols (e.g., MCP, agent-to-agent exchanges).

\subsection{Capability Discovery and Contracts}
\label{sec:agents2-proto-discovery}
Advertise actions with names, schemas, pre/postconditions, data classifications, and audit requirements. Prefer machine-readable descriptors over prose documentation.

\subsection{Invocation Semantics and Governance Metadata}
\label{sec:agents2-proto-governance}
Attach governance metadata to each call: purpose, legal basis, user identity, privilege flags, jurisdiction, and retention directives. Ensure hooks for audit logging and downstream provenance.

\subsection{Error Handling and Escalation}
\label{sec:agents2-proto-errors}
Standardize error classes (authn/authz, validation, rate limits, data sensitivity) and define escalation policies compatible with Part III oversight modes.
