\section{Introduction and Scope}
\label{sec:llmD2-intro}

We address practical multimodal inputs common to legal and finance: preserving structure in PDFs, extracting tables and charts, interpreting images/screenshots, and summarizing audio. We also highlight privacy and redaction patterns.
