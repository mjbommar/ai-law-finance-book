\section{Publishing and Cataloging}
\label{sec:kg-foundations-publish}

Follow Data on the Web Best Practices for discoverability, provenance, quality, and licensing. Use DCAT to catalog datasets and endpoints. \autocite{w3c-dwbp}

\begin{keybox}[title={Checklist}]
\begin{itemize}
  \item License and access terms; contact points and update cadence.
  \item Machine-readable metadata (DCAT), JSON-LD contexts, and example queries.
  \item Versioning policy and deprecation notices.
\end{itemize}
\end{keybox}
