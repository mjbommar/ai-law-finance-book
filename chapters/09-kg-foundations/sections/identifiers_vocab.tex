\section{Domain Identifiers and Vocabularies}
\label{sec:kg-foundations-ids}

Knowledge graphs depend on durable identifiers and shared vocabularies.

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Entities:} LEI (ISO~17442) for legal entities (GLEIF), SEC CIK for US issuers/filers, ELI/ECLI for EU legislation and case law. \autocite{gleif-lei, sec-cik, ecli-ejustice}
  \item \textbf{Financial vocabularies:} FIBO as an OWL reference model; XBRL taxonomies (IFRS 2024, US GAAP 2025) for reporting concepts. \autocite{fibo-spec, ifrs-taxonomy-2024, fasb-usgaap-2025}
  \item \textbf{Provenance:} PROV-O for process lineage; align with the Evidence Record (\href{\#}{Chapter~3, Structured Outputs}). %TODO: update cross-chapter link to sec:llmC-evidence
  \autocite{w3c-provo}
\end{itemize}
